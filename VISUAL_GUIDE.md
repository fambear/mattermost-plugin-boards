## Color Scheme

### YouTube Preview
- Background: `#000` (black)
- Thumbnail: From YouTube CDN
- Play button: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.9)` (white, 90% opacity)
- Play icon: `#000` (black)
- Overlay: `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3)` (black, 30% opacity)

### Google Drive Preview
- Background: `linear-gradient(135deg, #4285f4 0%, #34a853 100%)`
  - Blue (#4285f4) to Green (#34a853)
- Icon: `white` with 80% opacity
- Play button: Same as YouTube

### Fullscreen Viewer
- Backdrop: `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.9)` (black, 90% opacity)
- Video background: `#000` (black)

## Animations

### Hover Effects
1. **Play Button Overlay**
   - Default: `opacity: 0` (invisible)
   - On hover: `opacity: 1` (visible)
   - Transition: `0.2s ease`

2. **Play Icon**
   - Default: `scale(1)`
   - On hover: `scale(1.1)` (10% larger)
   - Transition: `0.2s ease`

## Responsive Design

### Desktop (> 768px)
- Preview max width: 560px
- Viewer content: 90% width, max 1280px
- Close button: 20px from top/right

### Mobile (â‰¤ 768px)
- Preview: Full width
- Viewer content: 100% width and height

## Accessibility

### Keyboard Navigation
- `Tab` - Focus on video preview
- `Enter` or `Space` - Open fullscreen viewer
- `ESC` - Close fullscreen viewer

### ARIA Labels
- Play button: `aria-label="Play video in full screen"`
- Close button: `title="Close video"`
- Video preview: `role="button"` with `tabIndex={0}`

### Screen Reader Support
- Descriptive alt text for thumbnails
- Proper semantic HTML
- Focus management
