# Video Block - Quick Start Guide

## How to Add Videos to Your Boards

### Method 1: YouTube Videos

1. Copy a YouTube URL (e.g., `https://youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ`)
2. In your card, type `/video` or click the "+" button and select "Video"
3. Paste the YouTube URL
4. Click "Add" or press Enter
5. ‚úÖ Done! A preview with thumbnail appears

**Supported YouTube URL formats:**
- `https://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
- `https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID`
- `https://youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID`

### Method 2: Google Drive Videos

1. Upload your video to Google Drive
2. Right-click the video ‚Üí "Get link" ‚Üí Copy the link
3. In your card, type `/video` or click the "+" button and select "Video"
4. Paste the Google Drive URL
5. Click "Add" or press Enter
6. ‚úÖ Done! A preview appears

**Supported Google Drive URL format:**
- `https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view`

### Method 3: Upload Video File

1. In your card, type `/video` or click the "+" button and select "Video"
2. Click "Upload File" button
3. Select a video file from your computer (mp4, webm, ogg, etc.)
4. Wait for upload to complete
5. ‚úÖ Done! A preview appears

**Note:** Maximum file size is 500 MB

## How to Play Videos

### From Preview
- **Click** on the video preview
- **Keyboard**: Press `Enter` or `Space` when preview is focused

### In Fullscreen Viewer
- Video plays automatically when opened
- Use video controls to pause, seek, adjust volume
- **Close**: Click the X button, press `ESC`, or click outside the video

## Troubleshooting

### Video won't play
- **YouTube**: Check if the video is available in your region
- **Google Drive**: Verify sharing permissions are set correctly
- **Uploaded file**: Ensure the file format is supported by your browser

### Thumbnail not showing
- **YouTube**: The video might be private or deleted
- **Google Drive**: This is normal - Google Drive uses a placeholder icon
- **Uploaded file**: The video might still be loading

### Upload failed
- Check file size (must be under 500 MB)
- Verify file format is a valid video type
- Check your internet connection

## Best Practices

1. **Use YouTube for public content** - Better performance, no storage limits
2. **Use Google Drive for private content** - Control access with Drive permissions
3. **Upload files for offline access** - When internet connectivity is unreliable
4. **Keep file sizes reasonable** - Compress videos before uploading if possible
5. **Add context** - Use card description to explain what the video shows
